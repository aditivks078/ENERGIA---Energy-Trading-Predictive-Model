Energia operates in volatile wholesale electricity markets where:
Prices fluctuate significantly between trading windows
Renewable generation increases unpredictability
Market inefficiencies create short-lived arbitrage opportunities
Poor timing increases exposure volatility and erodes margins

To protect profitability while remaining competitive, trading strategies must be both accurate and risk-aware.

This project investigates the profitability and risk profile of arbitrage trading between the Day-Ahead Market and IDA1, evaluating:
Profit and loss performance
Exposure volatility
Strategic trading efficiency
